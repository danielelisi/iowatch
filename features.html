FEATURES

NFC 2.0 Technology

The iO watch has bring the proposed NFC 2.0 technology into reality. The new chip technology transmit data up to a maximum of 1.7 mbit/sec. With this speed, the watch can apply points, discounts, and process payments in seconds.

Along with the support GPS, the chip is intelligent enough to detect your exact location, so it will know exactly which and what merchant you're at.
 

Camera

The iO watch is the first smartwatch that hosts a 3 MP camera. It can take HD images up to the maximum resolution of 1980x1080. It supports auto focus, white balance, and has flash features. Now you can take pictures on the fly.

Biometric Sensors

The watch is protect with the fingerprint security. The LCD screen has a fingerprint sensor, where users will use to log on in order to access the main features of the watch. Without logging on, the watch is simply just a watch.

On the back of the watch, there is also a biometric sensors for heart rate monitoring. It can also detect the watch being removed from the user's wrist and upon doing so, it automatically logs the user out.

Wireless Charging

The watch comes with the trending wireless charging feature. It can fully charge the 1220mAh battery in approximately 30 minutes. With public wireless charging ports being installed throughout the world in coffee shops, malls, and libraries, your watch will never run out of battery.
